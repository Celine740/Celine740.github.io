Project Overview

  • Application and Purpose
    This website is designed as a personalized platform for showcasing Winnie Peng’s Garden and selling the plants she cultivates.

  • Intended Users
    Local residents of Winston-Salem.

  • Content Overview
    Winnie’s Garden is a small business that specializes in cultivating and selling a variety of plants. The website will feature a gallery of plants, a shop page, and an about page.

  • Page Design
    • Home Page
      Purpose: To introduce the website and provide navigation to other sections.
      Audience: All users.
      Content: Welcome message(Welcome to Winnie’s Garden!), navigation bars, garden photos
      Interactive Elements: Navigation links, a carousel for featured photos.
      Actions: Navigation, viewing featured content.
      Notes: Keep the design clean and inviting.

    • Plants Shop Page
      Purpose: Displaying all available plants for sale.
      Audience: Customers interested in purchasing plants.
      Content: Plants’ photos and descriptions of each plant, as well as pricing information.
      Actions: Viewing plant in details, adding to cart.

    • Plants Details Page
      Purpose: Providing information about a specific plant.
      Audience: Customers interested in buying a particular plant.
      Content: Plant description, pricing, and purchase option.
      Interactive Elements: Option to add to cart.
      Actions: Adding to cart, navigating back to plant showcase.

    • Check Out Page
      Purpose: Displaying all items in the cart.
      Audience: Customers ready to make a purchase.
      Content: Cart summary, delivery details.
      Data Entry: Yes (for delivery details).
      Validations: Ensure required fields are filled, validate email and phone number formats
      Actions: Completing purchase(Thank you for your order!), navigating back to plants showcase page.

    • About Page
      Purpose: Providing information about the business and the owner.
      Audience: All users.
      Content: A warm welcome message introducing Winnie Peng and her passion for gardening, contact information.
      Actions: Contacting the owner, learning about the business.

  • Dynamic Functionality:
    The photo gallery shows the pictures dynamically on the index page.
    Product detail page shows the products that user chosen
    A shopping cart system that updates the total amounts.
